METHODOLOGICAL INFORMATION

1. Description of methods used for collection/generation of data: 
This data was collected using researcher assisted survey; questions were asked of respondents in person, and entered into Qualtrics by members of hte research team. The data was downloaded from the Qualtrics platform as a .csv file.

2. Methods for processing the data: 
The data was processed by converting string values into ordered values; questions that had "check all that apply" responses were recoded into multiple variables to replace the concatonated set of string responses that are generated by Qualtrics. Some responses were removed from the data if they were not complete or appeard to be duplicates. 

3. There is no instrument- or software-specific information needed to interpret the data. 

4. Environmental/experimental conditions: Survey data were collected through intercept sampling, at public transportation locations and additional community locations.  

5. Quality-assurance procedures performed on the data: the data were entered by reserach assistants, and reviewed for duplicate responses.
